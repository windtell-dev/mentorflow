# The Loop

**Prepare → Teach → Observe → Adapt → Reflect → Repeat**

Every screen exists to serve one step of the loop, and every component answers one question: *what does the tutor need to know or do next?*

| Step | Screen | What happens |
|---|---|---|
| Prepare | Lesson builder | AI drafts a session plan grounded in the learner's memory; tutor edits |
| Teach | (offline) | The human part. The app stays out of the way |
| Observe | Reflection form | Two minutes: where they lit up, where they struggled, energy |
| Adapt | Learner profile | AI rewrites patterns, next-session moves, family summary |
| Reflect | Dashboard | Recent reflection resurfaces; streak quietly acknowledges the practice |
| Repeat | Dashboard | One primary action: **Prepare next session** |

## The compounding effect

Session 1 reflection produces generic-ish insight. Session 10's plan knows the pizza model works, the number line doesn't yet, energy fades at minute 40, and word problems need to be read aloud. **The product gets better the more you teach — that's the moat and the demo story.**
